1 Overview

✅ The Short Version: DocFlow is a free, browser-based PDF tool. We do not upload, store, or process your PDF files on any server. All file operations happen entirely in your browser on your device.

Welcome to DocFlow ("we," "our," or "us"). This Privacy Policy explains how we handle information when you visit and use our website located at docflow.app (the "Service").

By using DocFlow, you agree to the practices described in this Privacy Policy. If you do not agree, please discontinue use of our Service.

We collect two categories of information:

🚫

We collect nothing. Your files never leave your device. All processing is local.

📊

We collect anonymous usage data — page views, tool usage frequency, browser type, and country.

3 How DocFlow Works

🔒 Your files are 100% private. DocFlow uses client-side JavaScript libraries (PDF.js, pdf-lib, Tesseract.js) that run entirely within your web browser. No file data is ever transmitted to our servers or any third-party server.

When you use any DocFlow tool (merge, split, compress, OCR, sign, etc.):

  • Your file is read directly by your browser's JavaScript engine
  • All processing (compression, merging, signing, etc.) happens on your device
  • The output file is generated locally and downloaded directly to your device
  • No file content, metadata, or preview is sent anywhere

This is why DocFlow works even offline once the page is loaded.

7 Cookie Policy

Cookies are small text files stored on your device when you visit a website. DocFlow uses the following types of cookies:

  • Essential Cookies: Required for the site to function (e.g., dark mode preference saved in localStorage). These cannot be disabled.
  • Analytics Cookies: Used by Google Analytics to understand how users interact with our site (anonymous data only).
  • Advertising Cookies: Used by Google AdSense to show relevant advertisements. These track browsing behavior across websites.
🍪 Cookie Consent: By continuing to use DocFlow, you consent to our use of cookies as described above. You can manage or delete cookies through your browser settings at any time.

How to control cookies:

  • Chrome: Settings → Privacy and Security → Cookies
  • Firefox: Options → Privacy & Security → Cookies
  • Safari: Preferences → Privacy → Cookies
  • Edge: Settings → Privacy → Cookies

Note: Disabling cookies may affect the functionality of advertisements and analytics on our site.
